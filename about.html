THE SPACE WHERE
ART BREATHES.

Avakash — in Sanskrit, means space. Room. Possibility. We created Avakash because we believe every person deserves the space to discover classical art — regardless of where they live, how old they are, or how much experience they carry. Bharatnatyam is not a privilege of the few. It is a heritage that belongs to all of us.

Meet Your Guru

Traditional Mastery

Ekta Kamble is a dedicated classical Bharatnatyam dancer with formal academic training and years of performance and teaching experience. Her approach blends the strictness of traditional classical training with the warmth of a mentor who genuinely invests in every student's growth.

"At Avakash, she doesn't just teach Bharatnatyam — she lives it, and she passes that devotion on to every student who walks through these virtual doors."

Our Philosophy

"At Avakash, we follow the time-honoured GURU-SHISHYA PARAMPARA — the sacred tradition of a teacher who doesn't just instruct, but shapes."

Ekta doesn't simply demonstrate adavus; she observes you, understands your body, corrects your posture, and celebrates your progress. Every student at Avakash is seen.
